
Transcript 4: Persuasive Call in Banka
Sumitra Devi: Namaskar bhaiya, pranam. Hum Sumitra Devi bol rahi hoon, Rajesh Verma ji ke office se. Rajesh ji chahte hain ki hum aapke jaise logon se baat karein aur unki samasya samjhein. Thoda samay de sakte hain?

Villager: Haan ji, kahiye. Kya kaam hai?

Sumitra Devi: Bahut dhanyawaad. Main Rajesh Verma ji ke karyalay se bol rahi hoon. Unhone hi kaha hai ki hum gaon gaon phone karke asli dikkaton ko samjhein. Abhi hum Banka ke logon se sun rahe hain ki sabse bada mudda yahan Road conditions, Healthcare aur Employment hai. Aapke hisaab se sabse bada dikkat ka hai?

Villager: Ji, sabse bada dikkat hai Road conditions. Hamare yahan ki sadkein bahut kharab hain. Gaddi chalane me bahut pareshaani hoti hai, aur fasal ko bazar tak le jaane me bhi dikkat hoti hai.

Sumitra Devi: Ji haan, sahi kaha aapne. Hum aapka dard samajh sakte hain. Yeh samasya itni purani hai aur is par kisi ne dhyan nahi diya. Lekin Rajesh ji ne Sansad me ise uthaya hai. Unka kehna hai ki bina Road conditions ke hal ke, log bahut pareshaan rahenge. Unhone toh department se kaha hai ki iska turant hal nikala jaye. Humne bhi report banayi hai is par, Rajesh ji ne khud bola hai ki isme koi dheel nahi honi chahiye.

Villager: Lekin abhi tak to kuch hua nai hai didi. Bas baatein hi hoti hain. Hamara nuksaan har saal hota hai. Kya fayda?

Sumitra Devi: Haan, aap sahi bol rahe hain. Hum aapka gussa samajh rahe hain, aur aapka pareshaani bhi. Lekin abhi file department me hai aur Rajesh ji khud monitoring kar rahe hain. Unka ek hi maksad hai ki aap jaise kisanon ko sahi samay par rahat mile. Jaise abhi unhone Healthcare ka issue bhi uthaya – kyunki yeh gaon ke liye bahut zaruri hai. Sabko sahi samay par ilaaj milna chahiye.

Villager: Haan, Healthcare ka bhi dikkat hai. Har ghar me baat hota hai iske upar. Hamari family me koi beemar hota hai toh bahut door jaana padta hai, aur wahan bhi sahi ilaaj nahi milta.

Sumitra Devi: Bilkul, bilkul. Rajesh ji isi liye toh nayi clinics aur medical facilities ke liye sarkar se request kar rahe hain. Woh chahte hain ki aane waale samay me kisi bhi kisan ko ilaaj ke liye pareshaan na hona pade. Aur woh yeh bhi chahte hain ki Employment par logon ko turant fayda mile. Isliye woh subsidy aur scheme ko aage badhawa rahe hain. Aapke jaise logon ko naukri aur kaam milna chahiye.

Villager: Ee ta bahut accha baat hai. Par Rajesh ji gaon kab aawat hain? Hume unse milna hai aur apni baat rakhni hai.

Sumitra Devi: Ji, agle hafte unka karyakram hai Baheri market me. Aap wahan par aayiye, unse milne ka mauka milega. Aap apna sujhav bhi khud unko bata sakte hain. Woh aapke liye hi kaam kar rahe hain. Woh aapka haal sunne ke liye hi gaon aa rahe hain.

Villager: Theek hai didi. Hum milenge. Hum apne padosiyon ko bhi bata denge. Sabhi aayenge.

Sumitra Devi: Bahut accha. Hum likh liya – Banka me sabse bada mudda hai Road conditions, Healthcare aur Employment. Rajesh ji tak hum turant pahuncha denge. Aapke jaise logon ka sahyog unke liye bahut mahatvapurn hai.

Villager: Dhanyawaad didi.

Sumitra Devi: Ji, namaskar bhaiya. Aapke baat Rajesh ji ko batayenge.

Villager: Namaskar.
